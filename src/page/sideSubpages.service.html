Service & Maintenance

The auto gyro is not a very maintenance intensive aircraft, yet it requires special attention, since we do not carry life saving system on board. We offer a variety of maintenance services in collaboration with our maintenance partners. The necessary servicing must be conducted by competent personnel to ensure safety, with some parts of the gyrocopter not readily accessible.

25/100h Check-up

We instruct a regular maintenance check according to our maintenance list and handbook as following: after 25 hours, after each 100 hours and once per year, whichever occurs first. Our maintenance list is available for our worldwide partners, who are able to operate all service and maintenance works for you. Within regular seminars, our partners are trained to qualify and maintain their technical qualification.

Some parts which are prone to wear and tear should be replaced regularly to maintain the reliability and value of your machine; replacement parts will include the latest updates and improvements.

Annual Inspection

An annual inspection is required by the Aviation Act, and must be performed annually. We gladly perform this inspection in house and would need you to bring the following documents:

 

• Registration papers

• Insurance papers

• Noise evaluation

• Test protocol (last time)

• Test certificate (last time)

• Weight and balance (last time)

• Avionics protocol (last time)

• EASA Form 1 (only with built-in radio / transponder)

• Registration with the Federal Network Agency
