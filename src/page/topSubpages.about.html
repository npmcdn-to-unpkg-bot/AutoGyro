About us

The AutoGyro GmbH is a world leader in the development, production and distribution of gyroplanes.

Since its inception in 1999, the specialist in air exercise equipment is growing steadily and has now a team of more than 100 highly qualified specialists and engineers.

Since 2007 the company is producing gyros in series and is able to put together one gyroplane per day. With distributors in nearly 30 countries, AutoGyro utilizes a global network with the aim to limitless implement the joy of flying in a safe and versatile gyro.
