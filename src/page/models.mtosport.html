MTOsport - experience freedom

The MTOsport is the logical development of our successful MT03. There are now more than 800 gyroplanes used successfully worldwide. As the first in the UL- class approved gyro in Germany, the MT03 offers a whole new flying experience. The MTOsport was introduced in spring 2008.

To adapt the gyro to various situations around the world, we offer floats to perform takeoffs and landings on water. Thus, the application for the MTOsport is almost limitless.

Technical Specifications of the MTOsport:

Length x width x height: 5,1 m x 1,9 m (1,6m) x 2,7 m
Rotor diameter: 8,0 or 8,4 m
Cruising speed: 160 km/h
Maximum speed: 185 km/h
Rate of climb: 4 m/s
Max takeoff weight: 450 / 560 kg
Motor: Rotax 912 ULS (100 hp) or Rotax 914 UL (Turbo with 115 hp),the two motors each with reduction gearbox 1:2,43
